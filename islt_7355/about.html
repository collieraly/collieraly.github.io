About Me

I have worked full-time in higher education for more than 9 years. Throughout my career, I’ve had a variety of roles. I have guided high school students through the admissions process. I have helped seniors develop resumes and write cover letters for their future jobs. I have even helped change a manikin into a new hospital gown before simulation training.

Now, I spend my days answering emails, calls and chat messages about technology. I currently work as an Instructional Technologist for Missouri Online.

Education:

  • Master of Arts, Higher Education and Administration, University of Missouri-Kansas City
  • Bachelor of Arts, Communication, Emphasis in Integrated Marketing Communications, Wichita State University
