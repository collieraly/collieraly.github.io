About Me

I have a Master of Arts in Higher Education Administration and just started an Educational Specialist degree in Learning Technologies and Design through the University of Missouri. I expect to complete it in the spring of 2024. Throughout my career, I’ve had a variety of roles in higher education. I have guided high school students through the admissions process. I have helped seniors develop resumes and write cover letters for their future jobs. I have even helped change a manikin into a new hospital gown before simulation training.

Now, I spend my days answering emails, calls and chat messages about technology. I currently work as an Instructional Technologist for Missouri Online.

You can learn more about each of these roles on my experience page. Or you can check out my full resume here or view my video resume.
